Getting Started

These steps will help you integrate IDP-ASE into your transcriptomics analysis pipeline.

  • Read the IDP-ASE requirements for running IDP-ASE.
  • Download and set-up the IDP-ASE package.
  • Follow the guide for using IDP-ASE.
  • Go through the tutorial example for IDP-ASE.
  • You're ready, Happy IDPing!
